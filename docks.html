Dock Construction

Custom Docks That Define Your Waterfront

Your dock is more than a place to tie up your boat — it's an extension of your home, a gathering place, and a statement about how you live on the water. Contour Marine builds docks that are as beautiful as they are functional, engineered to withstand South Florida's saltwater environment for decades to come.

Every dock we build starts with a deep dive into your needs — your vessel, your lifestyle, your property. We design custom dock systems that maximize your waterfront, improve access, and elevate the look of your entire property.

  • New dock construction — floating & fixed
  • Hardwood and composite decking options
  • L-shaped, T-shaped, and straight dock designs
  • Cleats, bumpers, and dock hardware
  • Full permitting — we handle it all

Materials & Design

Marine-Grade Materials for a Lifetime of Performance

We source only the finest marine-grade materials — from pressure-treated Ipe hardwood to premium composite decking systems designed to resist rot, warping, and saltwater corrosion. Every fastener, every bracket, every plank is selected for South Florida's demanding environment.

Our craftsmanship is evident in the details — clean lines, perfect level, precise spacing. Whether you want a natural hardwood aesthetic or a low-maintenance composite deck, we deliver exactly the dock you've been dreaming of.

  • Ipe and tropical hardwood options
  • Low-maintenance composite decking
  • Stainless steel & hot-dipped galvanized hardware
  • Concrete & treated timber pilings
  • Custom railing and cable rail systems
  • Boat lift integration available
