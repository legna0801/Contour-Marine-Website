Deck Construction

Luxury Outdoor Living — Engineered for the Coast

A well-designed waterfront deck doesn't just add square footage — it adds a completely new dimension to your lifestyle. Contour Marine builds decks that blur the line between your indoor living space and the sparkling water beyond, creating outdoor environments worthy of a luxury coastal estate.

Whether you're looking for an elevated pool deck, a waterfront entertainment space, or a seamless connection between your home and dock, our team designs and builds it with the same care and precision we bring to every marine structure.

  • Pool deck and entertainment area design
  • Composite, hardwood & concrete deck surfaces

Design & Materials

Surfaces That Elevate Your Property's Value

We work with the finest deck materials — from exotic Ipe hardwood and Teak to industry-leading composite systems like Trex and TimberTech. Each surface is selected based on your aesthetic vision, maintenance preferences, and the demands of South Florida's saltwater environment.

Our concrete deck work is also second to none — clean-finish concrete with precision edging, integrated drainage, and that unmistakable luxury look you see at South Florida's finest waterfront properties.

  • Ipe, Teak & exotic hardwood decking
  • Trex & TimberTech composite systems
  • Clean-finish concrete deck surfaces
  • Porcelain tile outdoor deck options
  • Drainage, waterproofing & coatings
  • Built-in seating and planters
